Introduction

In addition to the design assignments in CS1300, I also worked on assignments focused on website functionality. I built a rudimentary bakery e-commerce website for this specific assignment.

Background

Many major e-commerce websites like Amazon and Target have functionality to show customers the items in their cart and the total cost of the cart's items.

Users expect a list of items and total item of items due to existing e-commerce websites. For my website, I took into account existing user expectations to display a cart list and cart price for my bakery website.

Implementation

I used React, Javascript, and CSS to create the website. I was more focused on functionality, so the website's design is not refined.
In this assignment, I used React props and components to dynamically calculate and display the items in the cart and the cart's price.
